Who We Are

Located on Franklin Street in the heart of Chapel Hill, Carolina Coffee shop has been serving Tar Heels for almost a century. We are locally owned and pride ourselves in providing classic Tar Heel drinks and dishes. Carolina Coffee Shop is the perfect place to sit down and enjoy with your friends and family.

Join us and create your own memories with us while you are in historic Chapel Hill!
